Hi! I’m Rachel, and this is a small sampling of the nerdy, handmade projects I’ve created over the past few years. I originally picked up fiber arts during the early COVID-19 pandemic as a way to pass the time while we were all stuck indoors. That spark quickly grew into a full creative hobby. Since then, I’ve made everything from crochet and cross-stitch pieces to custom crafts, gifts, and other hands-on creations.
